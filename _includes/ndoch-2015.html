National Day Of Civic Hacking 2015

6th June, Music City Center 8am-8pm

Afterparty 8pm-10pm at Music City Center hosted by Eventbrite

Join Thousands of civic hackers from across the country and help build solutions for our communities and local government by using open data, technology and creative design processes.
